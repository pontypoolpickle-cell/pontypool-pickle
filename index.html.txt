                    <h2 class="text-5xl font-black mb-6 border-b-4 border-rose-600 pb-2 italic">Standard Rules</h2>
                    
                    <details><summary>Basic Rules Overview</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>Pickleball is played either as doubles (two players per team) or singles; doubles is most common. The same size playing area and rules are used for both singles and doubles.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>
                    
                    <details><summary>The Serve</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• The server’s arm must be moving in an upward arc when the ball is struck.</p>
                            <p>• Paddle contact with the ball must not be made above the waist level.</p>
                            <p>• The head of the paddle must not be above the wrist at contact.</p>
                            <p>• <strong>Foot Follow-through:</strong> At the time the ball is struck, the server’s feet may not touch the court. You may follow through into the court area only after striking the ball. If your foot is over the line before the ball is struck, it is a fault and the turn is over.</p>
                            <p>• The serve is made diagonally crosscourt and must land within the confines of the opposite diagonal court.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Service Sequence</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• Both players on the serving doubles team have the opportunity to serve until they commit a fault.</p>
                            <p>• The first serve of each team is made from the right-hand court.</p>
                            <p>• If a point is scored, the server switches sides and initiates the next serve from the left-hand court.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Scoring</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• Pickleball scoring uses a 3-number system for doubles (e.g., 4-2-1): your team's score, the opposing team's score, and your server number (1 or 2)</p>
                            <p>• Points are scored only by the serving team.</p>
                            <p>• Games are normally played to 11 points, win by 2.</p>
                            <p>• Tournament games may be to 15 or 21, win by 2.</p>
                            <p>• When the serving team’s score is even (0, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10), the player who was the first server in the game for that team will be in the right/even court when serving or receiving; when odd (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) that player will be in the left/odd court when serving or receiving.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Two-Bounce Rule</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• When the ball is served, the receiving team must let it bounce before returning, and then the serving team must let it bounce before returning, thus two bounces.</p>
                            <p>• After the ball has bounced once in each team’s court, both teams may either volley the ball (hit the ball before it bounces) or play it off a bounce (ground stroke).</p>
                            <p>• The two-bounce rule eliminates the serve and volley advantage and extends rallies.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Line Calls</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• A ball contacting any part of any line, except the non-volley zone line on a serve, is considered “in.”</p>
                            <p>• A serve contacting the non-volley zone line is short and a fault.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Non-Volley Zone or Kitchen</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• The non-volley zone is the court area within 7 feet on both sides of the net.</p>
                            <p>• Volleying is prohibited within the non-volley zone. This rule prevents players from executing smashes from a position within the zone.</p>
                            <p>• It is a fault if, when volleying a ball, the player steps on the non-volley zone, including the line and/or when the player’s momentum causes them or anything they are wearing or carrying to touch the non-volley zone including the associated lines.</p>
                            <p>• It is a fault if, after volleying, a player is carried by momentum into or touches the non-volley zone, even if the volleyed ball is declared dead before this happens.</p>
                            <p>• A player may legally be in the non-volley zone any time other than when volleying a ball.</p>
                            <p>• The non-volley zone is commonly referred to as “the kitchen.”</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Faults</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content space-y-2">
                            <p>• A fault is any action that stops play because of a rule violation.</p>
                            <p>• A fault by the receiving team results in a point for the serving team.</p>
                            <p>• A fault by the serving team results in the server’s loss of serve or side out.</p>
                        </div>
                    </details>

                    <details><summary>Determining Serving Team</summary>
                        <div class="rule-content">
                            Any fair method can be used to determine which player or team has first choice of side, service, or receive. (Example: coin flip)
                        </div>
                    </details>

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">I've never played, can I join in the fun?</summary>

                    <div class="rule-content">
                        Not at all! We know life gets busy, which is exactly why we do our best to organize sessions and events on different days of the week. Our schedule is designed to fit around various diaries and lifestyles, so you can just dip in and play whenever it suits you.

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">Can children play?</summary>
                    <div class="rule-content space-y-3">
                        <p>Because the club is in its early development stages, we don't currently offer specific sessions or coaching for children. Ensuring the safety and proper safeguarding of young people is incredibly important to us, so establishing a dedicated junior section is something we plan to look into once the club is fully established.</p>

If you'd like to help us get a youth programme off the ground, please visit our Contact Us page to express your interest. For the time being, our sessions are open exclusively to adults (ages 18 and over).</p>

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">Can I play against whoever I want?</summary>
                    <div class="rule-content space-y-3">
                        <p>During our dedicated Ladder Sessions, unfortunately not. To keep things fair and competitive, the ladder system automatically pairs you with players of a similar or slightly higher skill level. This structure encourages consistent challenges on the court, helping you test your limits and steadily improve your game. It also ensures that everyone gets high-quality matches and that our court time is utilized as effectively as possible for the whole club.</p>
                        <p>However, we love social, relaxed play just as much as competitive matches! While our current focus is on the ladder league, we hope to host Open Sessions in the future where you can mix, match, and play against whoever you like just for fun, without the scores affecting your online club rating.</p>
                    </div>
                </details>

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">What if I'm simply not good enough to play?</summary>
                    <div class="rule-content space-y-3">
                        <p>There is no such thing as "not good enough" at Pontypool Pickle—we've completely got you covered!</p>
                        <p>To help build your confidence, we use a ladder system. This ensures you are always paired with and playing against people of a similar skill level. Here is how it works:</p>
                        <p><strong>A Welcoming Start:</strong> If you are new to the club, you will automatically start with a base score of 1200.</p>
                        <p><strong>No Pressure:</strong> This starting score places you comfortably in our introductory rankings so you won't ever feel overwhelmed on court.</p>
                        <p><strong>Find Your Groove:</strong> The more games you play, the more accurate your rating becomes. As you win games and your confidence grows, you will "climb the ladder" to play on higher courts!</p>
                    </div>
                </details>

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">How does the rating system work?</summary>
                    <div class="rule-content space-y-3">
                        <p>We use a dynamic rating system based on the traditional Elo method (similar to how global DUPR ratings operate) to ensure you are always playing against people of a similar skill level.</p>
                        <p><strong>Your Starting Block:</strong> When you create your account, you'll self-select your starting level based on your background or existing 4DR/DUPR rating (ranging from 1000 for beginners up to 1800 for Elite players). This ensures you land on the right court from day one.</p>
                        <p><strong>The Provisional Period:</strong> Your first 10 matches are "provisional." During this time, the system applies a boosted multiplier to your points so it can quickly find your natural level on the ladder.</p>
                        <p><strong>Every Point Counts:</strong> Once settled, your rating shifts after every match. Winning by a wide margin earns you more points than scraping a close 11-9 win. The same applies to losses.</p>
                        <p><strong>The Underdog Advantage:</strong> Beating a higher-rated player gives your ranking a massive boost! Flip side? If you lose to a heavily favoured, higher-ranked player, your score barely takes a hit.</p>
                    </div>
                </details>

                <details class="bg-white rounded-lg shadow-sm">
                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">
                        Why does it say "Decaying" below certain players' ratings and what does it mean?
                    </summary>
                    <div class="rule-content space-y-3">
                        <p>If you see "Decay" next to a player's name on the leaderboard, it just means they've been away from the courts for a little while! Our leaderboard is dynamic and designed to reflect current, active skill. To stop inactive players from permanently holding top spots without playing, we use a "decay" system.</p>
                        
                        <p>If someone takes an extended break from the club, their ranking points will gradually drop until they return and play a match. Everyone gets a <strong>30-day grace period</strong> of inactivity. A player's points will only start to drop—and the "Decay" tag will only appear—if they haven't played a match at the club for more than 30 consecutive days.</p>

                        <div class="bg-gray-50 p-4 rounded-lg border-l-4 border-rose-600">
                            <p class="font-black uppercase text-xs mb-2">The Inactivity Scale:</p>
                            <ul class="space-y-1 font-bold text-xs italic">
                                <li>• Week 1 of decay: -5 points</li>
                                <li>• Week 2 of decay: -10 points</li>
                                <li>• Week 3 of decay: -15 points</li>
                                <li>• Week 4 of decay: -20 points</li>
                            </ul>
                        </div>

                        <p>There are two safety nets in place to protect everyone's hard-earned scores:</p>
                        <p>1. <strong>The Absence Cap:</strong> The maximum total loss for any single period of absence is capped at 50 points. Once a player has lost 50 points, their score freezes.<br>
                        2. <strong>The Floor Limit:</strong> Decay will never drop a rating below 1200 points. If a player's score is already at or near 1200 when they go inactive, the decay stops automatically.</p>

                        <p>Playing just a <strong>single match</strong> instantly removes the "Decay" tag, stops the point drops, and resets the clock! However, any points lost during the absence are gone—they will need to win matches to climb back up. If they return and then go inactive again in the future, the 30-day clock restarts from zero.</p>
                        
                        <p>If a player is inactive for <strong>100 consecutive days</strong>, their profile will be automatically hidden from the active leaderboard to keep the standings fresh and clean for our regulars. Their points remain safely frozen, and their name will instantly reappear on the ladder as soon as they come back and play their first match.</p>
                    </div>
                </details>

                    <summary class="p-4 font-black uppercase text-sm md:text-base cursor-pointer hover:text-rose-600 transition">Do you have a downloadable app?</summary>

                        <p>In the meantime, our website is fully optimized for mobile devices. You can easily view our schedule, check player rankings, and secure your spot on the courts directly from your phone's web browser!</p>

        function renderStandings() {
            const standings = globalData.standings || [];
            const filtered = (currentGender === 'Everyone' ? globalData.standings : globalData.standings.filter(p => p.gender === currentGender))
            .filter(p => p.name !== "Singles" && !p.isHidden && !p.isUnder18 && (p.membership === "Member" || p.membership === "Admin"));
            
            const today = new Date().getTime();
            const thirtyDays = 30 * 24 * 60 * 60 * 1000;

            const rSorted = [...filtered].sort((a,b) => b.elo - a.elo);
            const ratingBody = document.getElementById('ratingBody');
            
            if (ratingBody) {
                ratingBody.innerHTML = rSorted.map((p, i) => {
                    // 1. Inactivity Check
                    let isInactive = false;
                    if (p.lastMatchDate) {
                        const lastDate = new Date(p.lastMatchDate).getTime();
                        if (!isNaN(lastDate)) isInactive = (today - lastDate) > thirtyDays;
                    }

                    // 2. Trend Logic (Force check both weeklySum and weeklyChange just in case)
                    const change = p.weeklySum || 0;
                    let trendHtml = '';
                    
                    if (change > 0) {
                        // Green Up Arrow
                        trendHtml = `<span style="color: #16a34a !important; font-size: 14px !important; font-weight: 900; margin-left: 6px;">▲</span>`;
                    } else if (change < 0) {
                        // Red Down Arrow
                        trendHtml = `<span style="color: #e11d48 !important; font-size: 14px !important; font-weight: 900; margin-left: 6px;">▼</span>`;
                    } else {
                        // Grey Dot for no matches this week
                        trendHtml = `<span style="color: #ccc !important; font-size: 14px !important; margin-left: 6px;">•</span>`;
                    }

                    return `
                    <tr class="hover:bg-gray-50 transition text-sm md:text-base">
                        <td class="px-2 py-4 md:p-6 font-black text-gray-400">${i+1}</td>
                        <td class="px-2 py-4 md:p-6">

        function submitAction(type) {
            // 1. Swap alert for triggerError
            if (!currentUser || currentUser.role !== 'Admin') {
                return triggerError("Admin access required.");
            }
            
            const btn = event.target; 
            if(btn.disabled) return;
            btn.disabled = true; 
            const oldTxt = btn.innerText; 
            btn.innerText = "Processing...";
            
            let d = { type: type }; 
            if(type === 'match') {
                d.matchDate = document.getElementById('matchDate').value;
                d.w1 = document.getElementById('w1').value; 
                d.w2 = document.getElementById('w2').value; 
                d.l1 = document.getElementById('l1').value; 
                d.l2 = document.getElementById('l2').value;
                d.sW = document.getElementById('sW').value; 
                d.sL = document.getElementById('sL').value;
                
                const unique = new Set([d.w1, d.w2, d.l1, d.l2]);
                if (unique.size < 4 || !d.matchDate || d.sW === "" || d.sL === "") { 
                    btn.disabled = false; 
                    btn.innerText = oldTxt; 
                    // 2. Swap alert for triggerError
                    return triggerError("Invalid data or duplicate players selected."); 
                }
            }
            
            google.script.run
                .withSuccessHandler((res) => { 
                    btn.disabled = false; btn.innerText = oldTxt; 
                    render(res); 
                    startAdminSession(); 
                    triggerToast("Saved!"); 
                    if(type === 'match') {['sW', 'sL'].forEach(id => document.getElementById(id).value = ""); } 
                })
                .withFailureHandler((e) => { 
                    btn.disabled = false; 
                    btn.innerText = oldTxt; 
                    // 3. Swap alert for triggerError
                    triggerError(e.message); 
                })
                .processAdminAction(d);
        }

        function generateScramble() {
            // 1. Get selected names from checkboxes (this is alphabetical)
            const selected = Array.from(document.querySelectorAll('.scramble-check:checked')).map(cb => cb.value);
            const btn = document.getElementById('genScrambleBtn');

            if(selected.length < 4) { 
                document.getElementById('minPlayersModal').classList.remove('hidden'); 
                return; 
            }
            
            // 2. RANDOMIZE HERE
            // We create a fresh copy, shuffle it, and assign it to our active global variable
            activeScramblePlayers = shuffleArray([...selected]);

            if (btn) {
                btn.disabled = true;
                btn.innerText = "Processing...";
                btn.style.opacity = "0.5";
            }

            let c1Count, c2Count;
            const totalCount = activeScramblePlayers.length; // Use the shuffled count

            // 3. SPLIT LOGIC (Using the now-randomized activeScramblePlayers)
            if (totalCount <= 7) { 
                c1Count = totalCount; 
                c2Count = 0; 
            } else if (totalCount <= 10) { 
                c1Count = Math.ceil(totalCount / 2); 
                c2Count = totalCount - c1Count; 
            } else { 
                c1Count = 6; 
                c2Count = totalCount - 6; 
            }

            const c1P = activeScramblePlayers.slice(0, c1Count);
            const c2P = activeScramblePlayers.slice(c1Count);

            const plan = [];
            // Build Court 1 Plan
            const t1 = ROTATIONS[c1Count] || ROTATIONS[4];
            t1.forEach((idx, r) => {
                const ps = idx.map(i => c1P[i]);
                plan.push({ round: r+1, court: 1, p1: ps[0], p2: ps[1]||"Singles", p3: ps[2]||"Singles", p4: ps[3]||"Singles" });
            });
            // Build Court 2 Plan
            if (c2Count > 0) {
                const t2 = ROTATIONS[c2Count] || ROTATIONS[4];
                t2.forEach((idx, r) => {
                    const ps = idx.map(i => c2P[i]);
                    plan.push({ round: r+1, court: 2, p1: ps[0], p2: ps[1]||"Singles", p3: ps[2]||"Singles", p4: ps[3]||"Singles" });
                });
            }

            // 4. Send the randomized plan to Google
            google.script.run
                .withSuccessHandler((res) => {
                    if (btn) { btn.disabled = false; btn.innerText = "Generate Rotation"; btn.style.opacity = "1"; }
                    render(res);
                })
                .withFailureHandler((err) => {
                    alert("Error: " + err.message);
                    if (btn) { btn.disabled = false; btn.innerText = "Generate Rotation"; btn.style.opacity = "1"; }
                })
                .startLiveScramble(plan);
        }

        function openProfile(playerName) {
            const p = globalData.standings.find(x => x.name === playerName);
            if (!p) return;

            // 1. Calculate Rating Rank
            const ratingSorted = [...globalData.standings].sort((a,b) => b.elo - a.elo);
            const skillRank = ratingSorted.findIndex(x => x.name === playerName) + 1;

            // 2. Calculate Advanced Stats from History
            let partners = {}, nemeses = {}, victims = {};
            let recentForm = [];
            let currentStreak = 0;
            let streakBroken = false;
            
            // For reliable matching, we compare everything in lowercase with no spaces
            const pKey = p.name.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '');

            // Process history (Matches are already reversed: Newest first)
            globalData.history.forEach(m => {
                const mw1 = m.w1.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '');
                const mw2 = m.w2.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '');
                const ml1 = m.l1.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '');
                const ml2 = m.l2.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '');

                const isWinner = (mw1 === pKey || mw2 === pKey);
                const isLoser = (ml1 === pKey || ml2 === pKey);
                
                if (isWinner || isLoser) {
                    // Build Recent Form (Last 5)
                    if(recentForm.length < 5) recentForm.push(isWinner ? 'W' : 'L');

                    // Calculate Current Unbeaten Streak
                    if (!streakBroken) {
                        if (isWinner) currentStreak++;
                        else streakBroken = true;
                    }
                    
                    // Partner and Opponent Insights
                    if (isWinner) {
                        const partner = mw1 === pKey ? m.w2 : m.w1;
                        if(partner !== "Singles") partners[partner] = (partners[partner] || 0) + 1;
                        
                        [m.l1, m.l2].forEach(opp => { if(opp !== "Singles") victims[opp] = (victims[opp] || 0) + 1; });
                    } else {
                        [m.w1, m.w2].forEach(opp => { if(opp !== "Singles") nemeses[opp] = (nemeses[opp] || 0) + 1; });
                    }
                }
            });

            const getTop = (obj) => Object.keys(obj).length ? Object.keys(obj).reduce((a, b) => obj[a] > obj[b] ? a : b) : "N/A";
            const dreamPartner = getTop(partners);
            const nemesis = getTop(nemeses);
            const favoriteOpponent = getTop(victims);

            // 3. Inactivity Decay Logic
            let decayText = "Status: Active";
            let decayColor = "text-green-600";
            if (p.lastMatchDate) {
                const daysInactive = Math.floor((new Date() - new Date(p.lastMatchDate)) / 86400000);
                if (daysInactive > 23) { 
                    decayText = (30 - daysInactive) + " Days until Decay";
                    decayColor = "text-orange-500";
                }
                if (daysInactive > 30) {
                    decayText = "Rating Decaying";
                    decayColor = "text-rose-600";
                }
            }

            // 4. Badge Logic
            let badgeClass = "badge-novice", badgeText = "Novice";
            if (p.elo > 1550) { badgeClass = "badge-pro"; badgeText = "Grandmaster"; }
            else if (p.elo > 1400) { badgeClass = "badge-elite"; badgeText = "Elite"; }
            else if (p.elo > 1250) { badgeClass = "badge-challenger"; badgeText = "Challenger"; }

            const winPct = p.totalGames > 0 ? Math.round((p.wins / p.totalGames) * 100) : 0;
            
            // 5. Inject Header
            document.getElementById('profileHeaderTitle').innerHTML = `
                <div class="flex items-center gap-2 mb-1">
                    <span class="skill-badge ${badgeClass}" style="font-size: 9px; padding: 3px 10px;">${badgeText}</span>
                    <span class="bg-rose-600 px-2 py-0.5 rounded text-[10px] font-black uppercase italic text-white">Rank #${skillRank}</span>
                </div>
                <h2 class="text-3xl font-black italic tracking-tight uppercase text-white">${p.name}</h2>
            `;

            // 6. Inject Body
            document.getElementById('profileContent').innerHTML = `
                <div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-4">
                    <div class="stat-card border-l-4 border-black p-3 text-left bg-gray-50 rounded-xl">
                        <p class="text-[10px] font-bold text-gray-400 uppercase">Skill Rating</p>
                        <p class="text-3xl font-black text-black">${p.elo}</p>
                        <p class="text-[9px] font-bold uppercase ${decayColor}">${decayText}</p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="stat-card border-l-4 border-rose-600 p-3 flex flex-col justify-center bg-gray-50 rounded-xl text-center">
                        <p class="text-[10px] font-bold text-gray-400 uppercase">Recent Form</p>
                        <div class="flex justify-center gap-1 mt-1">
                            ${recentForm.length > 0 ? recentForm.map(r => {
                                // FORCED COLORS FOR VISIBILITY
                                const bgColor = r === 'W' ? '#000000' : '#e11d48';
                                return `<span style="width: 24px; height: 24px; display: flex; align-items: center; justify-content: center; border-radius: 4px; background-color: ${bgColor} !important; color: white !important; font-size: 10px; font-weight: 900;">${r}</span>`;
                            }).join('') : '<span class="text-gray-300 italic text-xs">No matches</span>'}
                        </div>
                        <p class="text-[8px] text-gray-400 uppercase mt-1 font-bold tracking-widest">Last 5 Games</p>
                    </div>
                </div>

                <div class="grid grid-cols-3 gap-3">
                    <div class="py-3 bg-gray-50 rounded-xl text-center border border-gray-100 shadow-sm">
                        <p class="text-[9px] font-bold text-gray-400 uppercase">Win Rate</p>
                        <p class="text-xl font-black text-black">${winPct}%</p>
                    </div>
                    <div class="py-3 bg-gray-50 rounded-xl text-center border border-gray-100 shadow-sm">

                        <p class="text-[9px] font-black uppercase tracking-tighter" style="color: #e11d48 !important;">Current Streak</p>

                const names = matches.flatMap(r => [r[1], r[2], r[3], r[4]]);
                return [...new Set(names)].filter(n => n !== "Singles" && n !== "");
            };

            const c1Roster = getRoster(c1);

            let widthClass = c2.length > 0 ? "w-full" : "max-w-2xl w-full"; 

            let html = `<div class="${containerClass}">`;
            
            // Court 1
            html += `<div class="space-y-4 ${widthClass}">
                        <div class="bg-black text-white p-3 rounded-t-lg font-black text-center text-sm uppercase">Court 1</div>

        function renderSittingText(roster, matchPlayers) {
            const sitting = roster.filter(name => !matchPlayers.includes(name));

            return `<span class="text-[9px] text-rose-600 font-bold uppercase italic">Sits out: ${txt}</span>`;
        }

        function validateLadderHeadcount() {
            const count = document.querySelectorAll('.ladder-check:checked').length;
            const msg = document.getElementById('ladderHeadcountMsg');
            const btn = document.getElementById('genLadderBtn');
            if (!msg || !btn) return;

            if (count === 0) {
                msg.innerText = "";
                btn.disabled = false;
                return;
            }
            if (count < 4) {
                msg.innerHTML = `<span class="text-gray-400">${count} selected — need at least 4</span>`;
                btn.disabled = true;
            } else if (INVALID_LADDER_COUNTS.includes(count)) {
                msg.innerHTML = `<span class="text-rose-600">${count} selected — can't split evenly into courts of 4/5. Add or remove one player.</span>`;

                msg.innerHTML = `<span class="text-green-600">${count} selected — ready to go!</span>`;
                btn.disabled = false;
            }
        }

            const selected = Array.from(document.querySelectorAll('.ladder-check:checked')).map(cb => cb.value);

            const totalRounds = parseInt(totalRoundsRaw);

            if (!totalRoundsRaw || isNaN(totalRounds) || totalRounds < 1) {
                return triggerError("Please enter the number of rounds.");
            }

                return triggerError("Please select a valid number of players (4, 5, 8, 9, 10, 12+).");

        function copyWhatsAppStandings() {
            // 1. Get and sort ALL players by ELO (Removed .slice)
            const standings = globalData.standings || [];
            const sorted = [...standings]
                .filter(p => p.name !== "Singles")
                .sort((a,b) => b.elo - a.elo);

            if (sorted.length === 0) return alert("No player data found.");

            // 2. Build the formatted text string
            let text = "🏆 *PONTYPOOL PICKLE RANKINGS* 🏆\n";
            text += "📅 _" + new Date().toLocaleDateString('en-GB', { day: 'numeric', month: 'long', year: 'numeric' }) + "_\n\n";

            sorted.forEach((p, i) => {
                let emoji = "🔹";
                if (i === 0) emoji = "🥇";
                else if (i === 1) emoji = "🥈";
                else if (i === 2) emoji = "🥉";
                
                // Formatted line for each player
                text += emoji + " *" + (i + 1) + ". " + p.name + "*\n";
                text += "      Rating: " + p.elo + "\n";
            });

            text += "\n🏓 _See the full history at our website!_";

            // 3. Copy to Clipboard logic
            const el = document.createElement('textarea');
            el.value = text;
            document.body.appendChild(el);
            el.select();
            document.execCommand('copy');
            document.body.removeChild(el);

            // 4. Visual Feedback
            triggerToast("Full Standings Copied!");
        }

        function shuffleArray(array) {
            let currentIndex = array.length, randomIndex;
            while (currentIndex != 0) {
                randomIndex = Math.floor(Math.random() * currentIndex);
                currentIndex--;
                [array[currentIndex], array[randomIndex]] = [array[randomIndex], array[currentIndex]];
            }
            return array;
        }

        var calendarCurrentMonth = new Date().getMonth();
        var calendarCurrentYear = new Date().getFullYear();

        function changeCalendarMonth(direction) {
            calendarCurrentMonth += direction;
            if (calendarCurrentMonth > 11) { calendarCurrentMonth = 0; calendarCurrentYear++; }
            if (calendarCurrentMonth < 0) { calendarCurrentMonth = 11; calendarCurrentYear--; }
            renderCalendar();
        }

        function renderCalendar() {
            const events = globalData.events || [];
            const monthNames = ["January","February","March","April","May","June","July","August","September","October","November","December"];
            const titleEl = document.getElementById('calendarMonthTitle');
            if (titleEl) titleEl.textContent = monthNames[calendarCurrentMonth] + " " + calendarCurrentYear;

            const grid = document.getElementById('calendarGrid');
            if (!grid) return;
            grid.innerHTML = '';

            const firstDay = new Date(calendarCurrentYear, calendarCurrentMonth, 1);
            const lastDay = new Date(calendarCurrentYear, calendarCurrentMonth + 1, 0);
            const today = new Date();
            today.setHours(0,0,0,0);

            let startOffset = firstDay.getDay() - 1;
            if (startOffset < 0) startOffset = 6;

            // Build event map keyed by date string
            const eventMap = {};
            events.forEach(event => {
                const dateStr = event[2];
                if (!dateStr) return;
                if (!eventMap[dateStr]) eventMap[dateStr] = [];
                eventMap[dateStr].push(event);
            });

            // Empty cells before first day
            for (let i = 0; i < startOffset; i++) {
                grid.innerHTML += `<div class="rounded-lg" style="height:80px;"></div>`;
            }

            // Day cells
            for (let day = 1; day <= lastDay.getDate(); day++) {
                const dateStr = calendarCurrentYear + '-' +
                    String(calendarCurrentMonth + 1).padStart(2,'0') + '-' +
                    String(day).padStart(2,'0');
                const dayEvents = eventMap[dateStr] || [];
                const isToday = new Date(calendarCurrentYear, calendarCurrentMonth, day).getTime() === today.getTime();
                const isPast = new Date(calendarCurrentYear, calendarCurrentMonth, day) < today;

                let cellStyle = '';
                let dayNumStyle = '';

                if (isToday) {
                    cellStyle = 'background-color:#000000; border:2px solid #e11d48;';
                    dayNumStyle = 'color:#ffffff;';
                } else if (isPast) {
                    cellStyle = 'background-color:#f9fafb; border:2px solid #f3f4f6;';
                    dayNumStyle = 'color:#d1d5db;';
                } else {
                    cellStyle = 'background-color:#ffffff; border:2px solid #e5e7eb;';
                    dayNumStyle = 'color:#111827;';
                }

                // Build event pills — max 2 visible, +N badge if more
                let pillsHtml = '';
                const maxVisible = 2;
                const visibleEvents = dayEvents.slice(0, maxVisible);
                const extraCount = dayEvents.length - maxVisible;

                visibleEvents.forEach(ev => {
                    const isPastEvent = new Date(ev[2]) < today;
                    const under18Only = !!ev[14];

                    let logoSrc, borderColor, opacity;
                    if (isPastEvent) {
                        logoSrc = 'https://i.postimg.cc/W1Wps66D/Logo-red.jpg';
                        borderColor = '#d1d5db';
                        opacity = '0.35';
                    } else if (under18Only) {
                        logoSrc = 'https://i.postimg.cc/zGtDqSSb/Logo-Blue.jpg';
                        borderColor = '#3b82f6';
                        opacity = '1';
                    } else {
                        logoSrc = 'https://i.postimg.cc/W1Wps66D/Logo-red.jpg';
                        borderColor = '#e11d48';
                        opacity = '1';
                    }

                    pillsHtml += `
                        <div onclick="event.stopPropagation(); openCalendarEventModal('${ev[0]}')"
                            title="${ev[1]}"
                            style="cursor:pointer; margin-top:3px; display:flex; align-items:center; justify-content:center;">
                            <img src="${logoSrc}"
                                style="width:22px; height:22px; border-radius:50%; object-fit:cover; opacity:${opacity}; border: 2px solid ${borderColor};"
                                alt="${ev[1]}">
                        </div>`;
                });

                if (extraCount > 0) {
                    pillsHtml += `
                        <div style="font-size:8px; font-weight:900; color:#e11d48; text-transform:uppercase; margin-top:2px; padding-left:2px; text-align:center;">
                            +${extraCount}
                        </div>`;
                }

                grid.innerHTML += `
                    <div class="rounded-lg p-1.5 flex flex-col overflow-hidden" 
                        style="height:80px; ${cellStyle}">
                        <span style="font-size:11px; font-weight:900; line-height:1; ${dayNumStyle}">${day}</span>
                        <div class="flex flex-col overflow-hidden flex-1 mt-0.5">
                            ${pillsHtml}
                        </div>
                    </div>
                `;
            }

            // Inject create event button for admins
            const adminBtn = document.getElementById('calendarAdminBtn');
            if (adminBtn) {
                if (currentUser && currentUser.role === 'Admin') {
                    adminBtn.innerHTML = `<button onclick="openEventModal()" class="bg-black text-white px-4 py-2 rounded-xl font-black uppercase text-xs hover:bg-rose-600 transition">+ Create Event</button>`;
                } else {
                    adminBtn.innerHTML = '';
                }
            }
        }

        function handleEventClickFromCalendar(eventId, asReserve) {
            if (!currentUser) { closeCalendarEventModal(); openAuthModal(); return; }

            const btn = event.target;
            if (btn) {
                btn.disabled = true;
                btn.innerText = "Processing...";
                btn.style.opacity = "0.6";
            }

            const fullName = currentUser.firstName + " " + currentUser.surname;
            const email = currentUser.email || "";
            const successMsg = asReserve ? "Added to Reserve List!" : "Registration Successful!";

            const onSuccess = (res) => {
                closeCalendarEventModal();
                render(res);
                triggerToast(successMsg);
            };

            const onFailure = (err) => {
                triggerError(err.message);
                if (btn) { 
                    btn.disabled = false; 
                    btn.innerText = asReserve ? "Join Reserve List" : "Sign Up"; 
                    btn.style.opacity = "1"; 
                }
            };

            if (asReserve) {
                google.script.run
                    .withSuccessHandler(onSuccess)
                    .withFailureHandler(onFailure)
                    .signupAsReserve({ eventId, name: fullName, dob: email, type: currentUser.role });
            } else {
                google.script.run
                    .withSuccessHandler(onSuccess)
                    .withFailureHandler(onFailure)
                    .signupForEvent({ eventId, name: fullName, dob: email, type: currentUser.role });
            }
        }

        function processSignup() {
            const isUnder18 = document.getElementById('regUnder18').checked;
            const consentAck = document.getElementById('regConsentAck').checked;

            if (isUnder18 && !consentAck) {
                return triggerError("Please acknowledge that you understand a consent form must be submitted before your account can be approved.");
            }

            const data = {
                firstName: document.getElementById('regFirst').value,
                surname: document.getElementById('regLast').value,
                email: document.getElementById('regEmail').value,
                username: document.getElementById('regUser').value,
                password: document.getElementById('regPass').value,
                gender: document.getElementById('regGender').value,
                startingElo: document.getElementById('regSkillLevel').value,
                isUnder18: isUnder18
                };

                const btn = document.getElementById('signupSubmitBtn');

                const confirmPass = document.getElementById('regPassConfirm').value;

                if (!data.username || !data.password || !data.firstName) {
                    return triggerError("Please fill in all required fields.");
                }

                if (data.password !== confirmPass) {
                    return triggerError("Passwords do not match. Please check and try again.");
                }

                // Frontend duplicate username check
                const usernameTaken = (globalData.standings || []).some(p =>
                    p.name.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '') === data.username.toLowerCase().replace(/\s+/g, '')
                ) || (globalData.pendingUsers || []).some(u =>
                    u[0].toString().toLowerCase() === data.username.toLowerCase()
                );

                if (usernameTaken) {
                    return triggerError("Sorry, that username is already taken. Please choose another.");
                }
            
            btn.innerText = "Submitting...";
            btn.disabled = true;

            google.script.run
                .withSuccessHandler(() => {
                    // Show the nice custom modal
                    triggerSuccess("Application Submitted", "An admin will review your account soon.");
                    
                    // Clear the form fields
                    ['regFirst', 'regLast', 'regEmail', 'regUser', 'regPass', 'regPassConfirm'].forEach(id => {
                        document.getElementById(id).value = "";
                    });
                    document.getElementById('regUnder18').checked = false;
                    document.getElementById('regConsentAck').checked = false;
                    document.getElementById('under18Fields').classList.add('hidden');
                    
                    btn.innerText = "Submit for Approval";
                    btn.disabled = false;
                    closeAuthModals();
                })
                .withFailureHandler(err => {
                    // 2. UPDATED: Use triggerError instead of alert for server errors
                    triggerError(err.message);
                    btn.innerText = "Submit for Approval";
                    btn.disabled = false;
                })
                .registerUser(data);
        }

        function processPasswordReset() {
            const identifier = document.getElementById('resetIdentifier').dataset.confirmed;
            const token = document.getElementById('resetToken').value.trim();
            const newPassword = document.getElementById('resetNewPassword').value;
            const confirmPassword = document.getElementById('resetConfirmPassword').value;
            const btn = document.getElementById('resetSubmitBtn');

            if (!token) return triggerError("Please enter your reset code.");
            if (!newPassword) return triggerError("Please enter a new password.");
            if (newPassword !== confirmPassword) return triggerError("Passwords do not match.");
            if (newPassword.length < 6) return triggerError("Password must be at least 6 characters.");

            btn.disabled = true;
            btn.innerText = "Updating...";

            google.script.run
                .withSuccessHandler(() => {
                    triggerSuccess("Password Updated!", "You can now log in with your new password.");
                    closeResetPasswordModal();
                    closeForgotPasswordModal();

                    document.getElementById('loginModal').classList.remove('hidden');
                    btn.disabled = false;
                    btn.innerText = "Set New Password";
                })
                .withFailureHandler((err) => {
                    triggerError(err.message);
                    btn.disabled = false;
                    btn.innerText = "Set New Password";
                })
                .resetPassword(identifier, token, newPassword);
        }

        function submitAccidentReport() {
            // Validation
            const submitterName = document.getElementById('arSubmitterName').value.trim();
            const injuredName = document.getElementById('arInjuredName').value.trim();
            const incidentDate = document.getElementById('arIncidentDate').value;
            const incidentTime = document.getElementById('arIncidentTime').value;
            const description = document.getElementById('arDescription').value.trim();
            const declaration = document.getElementById('arDeclaration').checked;
            const role = document.querySelector('input[name="arRole"]:checked');
            const location = document.querySelector('input[name="arLocation"]:checked') || document.querySelector('input[name="arLocationOther"]:checked');

            if (!submitterName) return triggerError("Please enter your full name.");
            if (!role) return triggerError("Please select your role.");
            if (!injuredName) return triggerError("Please enter the name of the injured person.");
            if (!incidentDate) return triggerError("Please enter the date of the incident.");
            if (!incidentTime) return triggerError("Please enter the time of the incident.");
            if (!location) return triggerError("Please select the location of the incident.");
            if (!description) return triggerError("Please provide a description of the incident.");
            if (!declaration) return triggerError("Please tick the declaration box before submitting.");

            // Gather injury types
            const injuryTypes = Array.from(document.querySelectorAll('input[name="arInjuryType"]:checked'))
                .map(cb => cb.value === "Other" ? "Other: " + document.getElementById('arInjuryOtherText').value : cb.value);
            if (injuryTypes.length === 0) return triggerError("Please select at least one injury type.");

            // Gather medical interventions
            const medicalInterventions = Array.from(document.querySelectorAll('input[name="arMedical"]:checked'))
                .map(cb => cb.value === "Other" ? "Other: " + document.getElementById('arMedicalOtherText').value : cb.value);
            if (medicalInterventions.length === 0) return triggerError("Please select at least one medical intervention.");

            // Gather location text
            let locationText = location.value;
            if (locationText === "Other") locationText = "Other: " + document.getElementById('arLocationOtherText').value;

            // Gather optional fields
            const continuedPlaying = document.querySelector('input[name="arContinuedPlaying"]:checked');
            const leftEarly = document.querySelector('input[name="arLeftEarly"]:checked');
            const venueNotified = document.querySelector('input[name="arVenueNotified"]:checked');
            const isUnder18 = document.getElementById('arUnder18').checked;
            const guardianInformed = document.querySelector('input[name="arGuardianInformed"]:checked');

            const data = {
                submitterName: submitterName,
                submitterContact: document.getElementById('arSubmitterContact').value.trim() || "Not provided",

                isUnder18: isUnder18 ? "Yes" : "No",

                courtNumber: document.getElementById('arCourtNumber').value || "Not specified",

                bodyPart: document.getElementById('arBodyPart').value.trim() || "Not specified",
                description: description,
                venueNotified: venueNotified ? venueNotified.value : "Not answered",
                medicalInterventions: medicalInterventions.join(", "),
                continuedPlaying: continuedPlaying ? continuedPlaying.value : "Not answered",
                leftEarly: leftEarly ? leftEarly.value : "Not answered",
                guardianInformed: isUnder18 ? (guardianInformed ? guardianInformed.value : "Not answered") : "N/A",
                guardianDetails: isUnder18 ? (document.getElementById('arGuardianDetails').value.trim() || "N/A") : "N/A",
                additional: document.getElementById('arAdditional').value.trim() || "None"
            };

            const btn = document.getElementById('arSubmitBtn');
            btn.disabled = true;
            btn.innerText = "Submitting...";

            google.script.run
                .withSuccessHandler(() => {
                    triggerSuccess("Report Submitted", "Your accident report has been submitted and a copy sent to the club. Thank you.");
                    closeAccidentReportModal();
                    btn.disabled = false;
                    btn.innerText = "Submit Report";
                    // Reset form
                    document.getElementById('arSubmitterName').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arSubmitterContact').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arInjuredName').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arUnder18').checked = false;
                    document.getElementById('arIncidentTime').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arBodyPart').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arDescription').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arAdditional').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arGuardianDetails').value = "";
                    document.getElementById('arDeclaration').checked = false;

                })
                .withFailureHandler(err => {
                    triggerError(err.message);
